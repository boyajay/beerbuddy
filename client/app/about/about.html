Learn about the different types of beers across the world.

Beer style is a term used to differentiate and categorize beers by factors such as flavor, strength, ingredients, production method or origin. The flavor includes the degree of bitterness of a beer due to bittering agents such as hops, roasted barley, or herbs; it also includes the sweetness from the sugar present in the beer.
